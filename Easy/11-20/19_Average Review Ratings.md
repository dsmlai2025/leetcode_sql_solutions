## ðŸ’» SQL Solution

```sql
SELECT 
  EXTRACT(MONTH FROM submit_date) AS mth,
  product_id,
  ROUND(AVG(stars), 2) AS avg_stars
FROM reviews
GROUP BY 
  EXTRACT(MONTH FROM submit_date), 
  product_id
ORDER BY mth, product_id;
